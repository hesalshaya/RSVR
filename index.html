Regulatory Scientific Verification Report (RSVR)

This tool allows for the verification of regulatory information related to the product or active ingredient, such as its presence in databases, the availability of sources, or the existence of similar products. It also supports the verification of certain regulatory classifications, such as being listed in restricted lists. The tool is designed to provide preliminary guidelines to help you expedite the verification process.
